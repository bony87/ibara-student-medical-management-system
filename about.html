About Ibara Student Medical System

This system was created by Boniface chelluget, a diploma graduate in ICT from Friends College Kaimosi.

The Ibara Student Medical Management System is designed to help schools manage student health records, appointment bookings, and medical services efficiently.

The system includes modules for student login, pharmacy payments, doctor login, admin approvals, and more.

Boniface completed his industrial attachment at Kisii University in 2022 and 2023, where he gained practical experience in IT systems.
